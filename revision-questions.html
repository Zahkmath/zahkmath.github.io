A fitness app records that Maria ran for 1 hour 42 minutes 30 seconds.

a) Write the total time in hours.

b) The app calculates calories burned using C=480t where t is time in hours.

b) Calculate the number of calories burned.

A cyclist travels 3.08 km. Write this distance in metres.

×

a) A delivery drone travels 4.75 km. Convert this distance into metres.

b)The drone travels at 12 m/s. Calculate time in minutes.

×

A student buys a laptop priced at $1250. The store offers an 18% discount during a sale.

Calculate the amount of the discount.

×

A store decreases the price of a bicycle by 12%. The original price was $850.

a) Calculate the amount of the discount.

b) After the increase, a 10% discount is applied. Find final price.

×

The intensity of light follows I = 150 d-2, where d is the distance in metres.

a) Rewrite the formula without a negative index.

b) Find the intensity when d=3.

×

The population of a town is estimated as 48 736.

a) Write this number correct to 2 significant figures.

b) State the percentage error introduced by this rounding.

×

A circle has radius r = 5 cm.

a) Find the exact area of the circle.

b) The circle is cut in half to form a semicircle. Find the exact perimeter of the semicircle.

Give all answers in exact form.

×

A metal rod is measured to be 12.4 cm to the nearest 0.1 cm.

a) Write down the lower bound and upper bound of the length of the rod.


b) The rod is cut into 3 equal pieces. Find the lower bound and upper bound of the length of each piece.


×

The mass of a bacterium is 0.00000045 grams.


a) Write this in standard form.

b) A sample contains 3.2×108 bacteria.

Find the total mass in standard form.

×

A car depreciates by $1800 each year. Its initial value is $24 000.

a) Write an expression for the value after n years.

b) Find the value after 8 years.

c) After how many years will the value be less than $10 000?

×

A student saves $50 in the first month. Each month she saves $15 more than the previous month.

a) Find the amount saved in the 10th month.

b) Find the total saved after 10 months.
